# F1 — View Story

## User Story

As a visitor,
I want to see all story sentences when I open the page,
So that I can read the current story before deciding to contribute.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

```gherkin
Scenario: Display all sentences in chronological order
  Given the story contains 3 sentences
  When the visitor opens the page
  Then all 3 sentences are displayed from oldest to newest
  And each sentence shows the author name
  And each sentence shows the time it was submitted
  And each sentence shows whether it was written by a human or by AI

Scenario: Display empty state when no sentences exist
  Given the story has no sentences
  When the visitor opens the page
  Then a message is shown indicating the story has not started yet
  And no sentence items are displayed

Scenario: Human sentence is labeled as human
  Given a sentence was submitted by a human named "Alice"
  When the visitor views the story
  Then that sentence displays "Alice" as the author
  And it is labeled as "Human"

Scenario: AI sentence is labeled as AI
  Given a sentence was generated by the AI
  When the visitor views the story
  Then that sentence displays "AI" as the author
  And it is labeled as "AI"
```

---

## User Story 2

As a visitor,
I want the AI to automatically start a story when none exists yet,
So that I always arrive to something interesting rather than a blank page.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (User Story 2)

```gherkin
Scenario: AI generates a story opener when the story is empty
  Given the story has no sentences
  When the visitor opens the page
  Then the AI automatically generates one opening sentence
  And that sentence is saved and displayed on the page
  And it is labeled as "AI"

Scenario: AI-generated opener is not repeated on subsequent visits
  Given the AI already generated an opening sentence
  When the visitor opens the page again
  Then no additional AI sentence is generated
  And the existing sentence remains displayed

Scenario: AI opener generation fails gracefully
  Given the story has no sentences
  And the AI service is unavailable
  When the visitor opens the page
  Then the empty state message is shown instead
  And no error is thrown to the visitor
```
